A walk for weather & climate science

Walking 250 miles from NYC to DC

From May 23 to June 3, I walked up to 52 miles per day, interviewing scientists from 10 research institutions between NYC and DC. Follow along my journey by scrolling below!

By Syl Foisy · June 12, 2026

The journey

Last spring, I set out from Central Park and walked to Washington, D.C. On foot, the whole way: 270.4 miles, to carry one simple message to the people who set its budget. Weather and climate science is worth funding.

Along the route I stopped to meet climate and weather scientists, sitting down with each of them to talk about the work they do and why it matters. Scroll through the stops below, and the map will trace the path with you.
